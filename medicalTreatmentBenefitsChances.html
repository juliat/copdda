Optimizing Medical Treatment: Chances of Benefits

More Control

Out of 100 patients with severe COPD, about 62 say that they felt their actions directly affected their health when working with their doctors to optimize medical treatment.

Easier for Caregivers

Out of 100 patients with severe COPD, about 71 have caregivers who say caring for them was easier before a transplant process than during a transplant process.
