If you take your medications, go to pulmonary rehab and wear your oxygen you may be able to do more than you are doing right now. It doesn't work for everyone. Even if you take your medicines, exercise and wear your oxygen, you could get sick and need to spend a long time sick in the hospital. There is chance that you may even die.
