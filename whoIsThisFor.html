Who is this website made for?

This website is meant for people who have a lung disease called Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD). Sometimes COPD is also called chronic bronchitis or emphysema.

You should use this website if you:

• Have COPD
• Wear oxygen when you are at rest

This website NOT meant for

• People who do not have COPD
• People with COPD who do not need to wear oxygen at rest.

￼The goal of this website is to help you decide what COPD treatment option is right for you. It focuses on two different options:

1. To become a lung transplant candidate
2. To be treated with medicine only and not be listed for a lung transplantation

The decision to continue your medicines for your COPD versus choosing to be listed for lung transplantation is a personal decision. There is no medically right or wrong answer. Your values and preferences about how you want to be treated for COPD are important in this decision.

This website will tell you more about the good and bad things about each treatment option.
