Optimizing Medical Treatment: Chances of Risks

Shortness of breath

Out of 100 patients with severe COPD, around 78 of them report shortness of breath on a daily basis and 32 of the 100 patients report having panic attacks “several times a week”.

Limited independence

Out of 100 patients with severe COPD, 80 report being homebound, 36 being chair- bound and 78 can’t drive.

Doesn't always work

Out of 100 patients with severe COPD, 78 of the 100 patients have to be hospitalized 3+ times per year and 24 of the 100 have needed to be put on a breathing tube per year.
